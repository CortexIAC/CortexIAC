## ⚡ What I'm Building

I’m working on **SynAura** — a system I’ve had in mind for years.

Something that doesn’t separate tools, workflows, and intelligence…
but brings everything into one place.

Not just another app.
Not just another framework.

> A unified computing environment.

---

## 🧠 SynAura

SynAura is a **local-first, modular system** designed to connect:

* development
* automation
* system control
* AI interaction

The goal is simple:

> reduce the gap between thinking and doing.

---

## 🧩 How It’s Built

Everything revolves around three ideas:

* **CORE** → execution, logic, intelligence
* **NODE** → modular expansion
* **SCALE** → automation + workflows

These aren’t modules.

They’re how the system *thinks*.

---

## 🚀 Where It's Going

SynAura is being built to evolve over time into something that:

* adapts to how it’s used
* connects systems instead of isolating them
* turns workflows into something seamless

> The longer it’s used, the more it becomes *your system*.
